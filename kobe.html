Kobe Bryant announced on the Players' Tribune on Sunday evening that he will retire at the end of the current NBA season. The five-time champion and two-time NBA Finals MVP is bringing the curtain down on a 20-year professional career, and David Beckham was among those to pay tribute. Beckham, who spent five years in Los Angeles with the Galaxy, took to Instagram to post a photo with the message "Heroes come and go, but legends are forever." Back in 2011, Beckham spoke about his love for Bryant on his Facebook page, admitting it was a privilege to watch him play from the floor. The former England skipper, who can still occasionally be seen courtside at Lakers games, added a heartfelt tribute to Black Mamba, giving credit to Bryant for his will to win and ability to inspire kids. One legend pays his respects to another..
